  • 2026-06-30 Venom: A Symbiote's Anatomy

    The Klyntar — the alien species to which Venom belongs — are one of the most biologically fascinating organisms in the Marvel universe. They are not parasites in the classical sense. They are obligate mutualists that went wrong. What Is a Symbiote? In biology, a symbiont is any organism that lives in close physical association with another. The relationship can be: Mutualistic — both parties benefit Commensal — one benefits, the other is unaffected Parasitic — one benefits at the other’s expense The Klyntar were designed to be mutualistic. Their original purpose, engineered by the cosmic entity Knull, was to bond with hosts and elevate them — sharing strength, healing, and the collective memory of every previous bond.
